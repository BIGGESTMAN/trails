Modifiers

~ Physical Attack Up (modifier_str_up) : Physical damage increased.
~ Physical Attack Down (modifier_str_down) : Physical damage reduced.
~ Physical Defense Up (modifier_def_up) : Incoming physical damage reduced.
~ Physical Defense Down (modifier_def_down) : Incoming physical damage increased.
~ Magical Attack Up (modifier_ats_up) : Magical damage increased.
~ Magical Attack Down (modifier_ats_down) : Magical damage reduced.
~ Magical Defense Up (modifier_adf_up) : Incoming magical damage reduced.
~ Magical Defense Down (modifier_adf_down) : Incoming magical damage increased.
~ Speed Up (modifier_spd_up) : Delay reduced and attackspeed increased.
~ Speed Down (modifier_spd_down) : Delay increased and attackspeed reduced.
~ Movement Up (modifier_mov_up) : Movement speed increased.
~ Movement Down (modifier_mov_down) : Movement speed reduced.

~ Sealed (modifier_seal) : Unable to attack or use crafts.
~ Muted (modifier_mute) : Unable to cast arts.
~ Burned (modifier_burn) : On fire, losing 3%% of max HP each second.
~ Insight (modifier_insight) : Evading physical attacks, up to once per second.
~ Passion (modifier_passion) : Rapidly gaining CP.
~ Frozen (modifier_freeze) : All actions are delayed by 0.6 seconds, and will lose 10%% of max HP when the freeze ends.
~ Confused (modifier_confuse) : Uncontrollable, and randomly attacking nearby units.
~ Nightmare (modifier_nightmare) : Disabled and unable to act; when the nightmare ends, a random non-sleep debuff will be applied for 3 seconds. Ends early if damage is taken.
~ Deathblow (modifier_deathblow) : Will die instantly if unit falls below 15%% of max HP.
~ CP Boost (modifier_cp_boost) : CP gain increased by 50%%.
~ Petrify (modifier_petrify) : Disabled and immune to magic. Taking physical damage will break the disable early and deal pure damage equal to 20% of unit's missing health.
~ Faint (modifier_faint) : Disabled and taking 50%% more damage.
~ Intimidate (modifier_intimidate) : Unable to generate CP.
~ Physical Guard (modifier_physical_guard) : Blocks a single instance of enemy physical damage along with any associated effects.
~ Magical Guard (modifier_magical_guard) : Blocks a single instance of enemy magical damage along with any associated effects.
~ Airgetlam Guard (modifier_guard_high_priority) : Protected from all damage and associated effects while inside Lammy's force field.
~ Turn Bonus: Critical (modifier_crit) : Next craft, art or basic attack will deal double damage or heal for double health.
~ Turn Bonus: Zero-Arts (modifier_zero_arts) : Next art will be cast instantly and for free.
~ Turn Bonus: Brute Force (modifier_brute_force) : Next offensive craft or basic attack will instantly unbalance the target.
~ Turn Bonus: Link Broken (modifier_link_broken) : Cannot form combat links with allies.
~ Turn Bonus: Unshatterable Bonds (modifier_unshatterable_bonds) : Combat links cannot be broken, and have full effect at all ranges.

~ CP (modifier_cp_tracker_cp) : Amount of CP available for crafts. Increased by dealing and recieving damage, and gained slowly over time.

~ Combat Link (modifier_combat_link_linked) : Linked with an ally, enabling unbalancing of enemies and special link techniques.
~ Enhanced Crafts Ready (modifier_combat_link_followup_available) : An enemy has been knocked off-balance by an ally, rendering them vulnerable to Enhanced crafts.
~ Unbalanced Level (modifier_unbalanced_level) : Beginning to become unbalanced -- at 100, will be vulnerable to Enhanced crafts. Rapidly decays if not increased for 8 seconds.
~ Unbalanced (modifier_combat_link_unbalanced) : Off-balance, and able to be targeted by Enhanced crafts.

~ Autumn Leaf Cutter Slow (modifier_autumn_leaf_cutter_slow) : Movement slowed by %dMODIFIER_PROPERTY_MOVESPEED_BONUS_PERCENTAGE%%%.
~ Autumn Leaf Cutter Slow (modifier_autumn_leaf_cutter_unbalanced_slow) : Movement slowed by %dMODIFIER_PROPERTY_MOVESPEED_BONUS_PERCENTAGE%%%.
~ Autumn Leaf Cutter Knockback (modifier_autumn_leaf_cutter_knockback) : Sent flying by Enhanced Autumn Leaf Cutter.
~ Arc Slash Stun (modifier_arc_slash_stun) : Unable to act.
~ Wind Path Speed (modifier_arc_slash_speedbuff) : SPD +50 and MOV +80.
~ Gale Disarm (modifier_gale_disarm) : Unable to attack.
~ Gale Mark (modifier_gale_mark) : Marked by Rean's attacks -- once marked twice, will be vulnerable to Gale.
~ Enhanced Gale Slow (modifier_gale_slow) : Movement slowed by 80%%, decaying over the duration.
~ Sword Ablaze (modifier_azure_flame_slash_sword_inflamed) : Rean's sword flares with blue flame, and will Burn enemies he deals damage to.
~ Casting S-Craft (modifier_azure_flame_slash_casting) : About to unleash Rean's ultimate ability, Azure Flame Slash.

~ Flamberge Silence (modifier_flamberge_silence) : Unable to use crafts.
~ Mischievous Blessing (modifier_blessed_arrow_mischievous_blessing) : Marked by Enhanced Blessed Arrow. Enemy attackers will heal and gain bonus CP.
~ Molten Rain Slow (modifier_molten_rain_slow) : Movement slowed by %dMODIFIER_PROPERTY_MOVESPEED_BONUS_PERCENTAGE%%%.
~ Heavenly Passion (modifier_heavenly_gift_cp_restore) : Gradually restoring CP to surrounding allies based on CP stolen from an enemy with Enhanced Heavenly Gift.
~ Judgment Arrow (modifier_judgment_arrow_pulled) : Sent flying by Judgment Arrow.
~ Judgment Arrow Stun (modifier_judgment_arrow_stun) : Unable to act.
~ Judgment Arrow Silence (modifier_judgment_arrow_silence) : Unable to use crafts.
~ Casting S-Craft (modifier_judgment_arrow_casting) : About to unleash Alisa's ultimate ability, Judgment Arrow.

~ Rapid Volley (modifier_rapid_volley_casting) : Barraging an area with bullets.
~ Rapid Volley Slow (modifier_rapid_volley_area_slow) : Movement slowed by %dMODIFIER_PROPERTY_MOVESPEED_BONUS_PERCENTAGE%%%.
~ Rapid Volley Slow (modifier_rapid_volley_bullet_slow) : Movement slowed by %dMODIFIER_PROPERTY_MOVESPEED_BONUS_PERCENTAGE%%%.
~ Enhanced Rapid Volley (modifier_rapid_volley_enhanced_casting) : Rapidly barraging an area with bullets, and still able to move, although slowed by %dMODIFIER_PROPERTY_MOVESPEED_BONUS_PERCENTAGE%%%.
~ Casting S-Craft (modifier_cross_raven_casting) : About to unleash Crow's ultimate ability, Cross Raven.

~ Buster Arm Knockback (modifier_buster_arm_knockback) : Sent flying by Buster Arm.
~ Force Field (modifier_ultimate_barrier_force_field) : Lammy is generating a force field around Millium, protecting all allies inside from harm.
