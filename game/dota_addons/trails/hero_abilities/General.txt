General
 
[-] Combat Links (combat_link) : Members of Class VII automatically form Combat Links with nearby classmates, allowing them to perform powerful techniques to support each other.\n\nPhysical attacks made while linked have a chance to Unbalance, enabling a unique follow-up attack from the linked partner.
 
Link Range (link_radius) : 500
%Base Unbalance Chance (unbalance_chance) : 20%
Followup Attack Window (unbalance_duration) : 3
Note0 (note0) : Links will break if Class VII members move more than 550 distance away from each other.
